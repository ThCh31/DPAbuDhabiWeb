Overview & Pricing

Embark on an electrifying week blending high-intensity workouts, exhilarating adventures, and thrilling aerial experiences. Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival and welcome at hotel, kickoff talk with Coach Fatima, strength training with Coach Dona, BioCharger recovery session, and healthy welcome dinner.
  • Day 2: Full-body training and breathwork, indoor skydiving at CLYMB Abu Dhabi, followed by karting thrills at Yas Marina Circuit.
  • Day 3: Upper body strength and wellness talk, then an afternoon of extreme rides at Ferrari World and evening slides at Yas Waterworld.
  • Day 4: Early morning desert safari and sandboarding, followed by a lower body strength session and adrenaline-filled afternoon at Adrenark Adventure.
  • Day 5: Morning gym session, followed by a trip to Wadi Adventure in Al Ain for white-water rafting, kayaking, and surfing.
  • Day 6: Full-body training and motivational session, then tandem skydiving over Abu Dhabi and a high-speed F1 driving experience at Yas Marina Circuit.
  • Day 7: Final breakfast and checkout, optional re-entry to Yas Waterworld or Warner Bros. World, with souvenir shopping before transfer to the airport.
