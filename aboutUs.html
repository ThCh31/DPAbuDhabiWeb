Who We Are

At Dpointgroup – Abu Dhabi Recharge, we redefine what it means to travel with purpose. We are a collective of wellness professionals, travel designers, fitness coaches, and cultural curators committed to creating experiences that energize, inspire, and elevate. Whether you're a solo adventurer, corporate team, wellness seeker, or content creator — we provide more than just a retreat. We deliver a journey that nurtures your body, sharpens your focus, and reconnects you with yourself.

What We Offer

  • Yoga & recovery therapies
  • Breathwork, biohacking & mindfulness
  • Cultural immersions & guided explorations
  • Creator-focused branding and content sessions

Why We Do It

We believe that true transformation happens when movement meets meaning. That the most powerful journeys happen not only across landscapes — but within ourselves. Our goal is to help every guest return home stronger, calmer, clearer, and more connected.

Our Vision

To become the leading destination for fitness-driven travel in the Middle East — where travelers come not just to relax, but to reset, grow, and thrive.
